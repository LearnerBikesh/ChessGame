About Project

It is an interactive and engaging online platform designed to bring the classic game of chess to life. With a focus on both human vs. human and human vs. AI gameplay, it offers players of all skill levels the opportunity to challenge themselves and others in strategic battles on the board.

Human Vs Human Mode

Challenge your friends or other players to a game of chess. Experience the thrill of competing against real opponents in real-time.Human vs. Human mode offers a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience for players looking to test their skills against others.

Human Vs AI Mode

Test your skills against various AI difficulty levels. From beginner to grandmaster,it offers a challenging experience for players of all abilities.Whether you're looking to practice your tactics or take on a formidable AI opponent, Human vs. AI mode provides a dynamic and engaging chess experience for players of all abilities.
